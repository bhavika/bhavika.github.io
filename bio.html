Bhavika lived in Mumbai, India most of her life. She earned her Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from University of Mumbai in 2014 and joined Capgemini as a Software Engineer. After a brief stint in the consulting domain building enterprise software applications & internal tools, Bhavika decided to pursue her interests in data science and artificial intelligence. She currently resides in Fairfax, VA where she is earning her MS in Computer Science from George Mason University. During her Master's degree, Bhavika continued to work on a number of interesting software & data problems. She is currently a GIS Programmer at George Mason working on a version of the OpenGeoportal. In the summer of 2017, Bhavika interned at Udacity on the Data team to democratize education. In addition to her professional pursuits, Bhavika is passionate about music, technology, politics & reading.
