Bhavika grew up in Mumbai, India. She earned her Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from University of Mumbai in 2014 and started her career at Capgemini as a Software Engineer. After a brief stint in enterprise software & consulting, Bhavika decided to pursue her interests in data science and artificial intelligence. She earned her MS in Computer Science from George Mason University in May 2018. Some of the domains she has worked in include edtech, music, GIS & more recently telecommunications. In addition to her professional pursuits, Bhavika is passionate about music, technology, ethical enterprise, politics & reading. She currently resides in Washington DC and is a Data Engineer at a stealth mode startup called Aurora Insight.
